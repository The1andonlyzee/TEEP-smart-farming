## ✅ Weekly Check (Do This Every Monday)

1. Double-click: `tools/check-status`
2. Look at the output - all green? You're done! ✅
3. If there are red warnings, see TROUBLESHOOTING_FLOWCHART.md

---

## 🎬 Creating Time-Lapse Videos

1. Double-click: `tools/create-timelapse`
2. Follow the prompts
3. Video will be saved in: `data/timelapse-videos/`

---

## 📦 Monthly Backup

1. Double-click: `tools/backup-data`
2. Connect external hard drive when prompted
3. Wait for completion message
